What is PCOS?

PCOS

Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) is a condition in which the ovaries
produce an abnormal amount of androgens, male sex hormones that are usually present in
women in small amounts.

  • Diagnosis
  • PCOS Diet
  • Treatment

Symptoms

  • Irregular periods (missed and light)
  • Excess body hair (hirsutism)
  • Weight gain, especially around the belly
  • Male-pattern baldness or thinning hair
